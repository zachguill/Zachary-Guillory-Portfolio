About Us

Hiking Simple was created by enthusiasts who felt overwhelmed by all of the information on the web that seemed to be written by experts for experts. We wanted a website that took all of the complicated information and made it accessible to both first-time hikers and experienced hikers. We strive to offer the best trail and gear recommendations on the web.
