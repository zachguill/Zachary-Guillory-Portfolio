Apparel

Qualtiy hiking apparel can last years and lead to a comfortable hike. Ensuring you are dressed for the weather, especially when climbing many thousands of feet in elevation usually means layering.

Jackets

While it may seem warm at the trailhead, temperatures drop significantly as you climb in elevation. It is important to take a jacket with you to ensure you have adequate protection from the elements for the entirety of your hike. Brands we trust are REI and Northface.

Pants

Pants or shorts may be appropriate depending on the hike. If you are taking a leisurely stroll on an urban hike, shorts may be the best choice. On difficult nature hikes, we almost always recommend pants. They will protect your legs from scrapes if you slip and fall and they can keep you warm at higher elevations. Additionally, in states with a high occurrence of Lyme disease, pants can protect you from ticks. Brand we trust are Mountain Khaki, Kuhl, and Outdoor Research.
